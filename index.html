What is REAPER? Test

REAPER(Rapid Environment for Audio Production,Engineering and Recording) is a digital audio workstation and MIDI Sequencer software. It is availible on Linux, Microsoft Windows(XP or newer) and macOS(10.5 or newer) The program allows users to create, modify and publish music tracks.

Why use REAPER?

REAPER comes with plugins built it to modify music. These plugins called ReaPlugs are free to download and are highly configurable to do exactly what the user wants them to do. If you don't find a plug-in you need within ReaPlugs, you can download hundreds of JSFX plug-ins which are also entirely customizable. These additional plugins feature plug-ins from simple audio effects (e.g delay, distortion, compression) to instruments themselves. You can also record audio yourself into REAPER if you have that instrument and equipment on hand. REAPER is compatible with the vast majority of free and commerial plug-ins availible. REAPER also allows importing of all commonly used media types: MP3, MIDI, Video etc.

Where can I get REAPER?

REAPER can be downloaded from their website if you have a license. There are two types of licenses: Discounted ($60) and Commerial($225). The discounted license is used if you are either an individual using REAPER for personal use, a business not making $20,000 yearly gross or an educational or non-profit orgranization. If you don't meet those requirements you must purchase the commerial license.
